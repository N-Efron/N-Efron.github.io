Furthermore, Also, Too By The Collective

The Collective, Baltimore’s hub for movers and shakers, presents a performance of dance premiers and current repertoire from the seventeen-member dance company focusing on transitional elements in life. How does change impact our perspective and inform dance making? How do we evolve, grow, and transition as individuals and as a dance company? Founded in 1999, The Collective Dance Company has evolved from a small group of motivated dance artists to a non-profit dance company in residence at the Bryn Mawr School. The company continues to evolve in order to meet the needs of local dancers and choreographers and to sustain the work of the organization. The Collective’s work is rooted in connecting the professional dance community and provide opportunities for local dance artists to make new work. Stay tuned to learn more about Furthermore, also, too, and find out ways you can support The Collective during leadership transitions and during the development of new, transformative work this season.
