Bare By Jon Hartmere and Damon Intrabartolo

Bare, the worldwide coming-of-age rock musical phenomenon, and one the most significant pieces of contemporary queer musical theatre, follows a group of high school renegades at a Catholic co-ed boarding school as they wrestle with their sexuality, their religion, and ultimately their identity. With deep honesty, alarming cultural relevance, and a gritty, alt-rock-pop score reminiscent of RENT and Spring Awakening, Bare delivers one of the most powerful and gripping theatrical experiences of this generation — an introspective into the fragility of our “bare” humanity. Please note that Bare explores mature themes, contains adult language, partial nudity, sexual content and simulated violence. Bare may not be suitable for patrons under the age of 16.
